About HomeTrendzz

12 years of design excellence, 500+ transformed homes, one unwavering mission — to make your dream home a reality.

Our Story

Born from a Passion for Beautiful Spaces

HomeTrendzz was founded in 2012 by architect and designer Riya Mehta, who believed that exceptional interior design shouldn't be reserved exclusively for the wealthy. Starting with a small studio in Noida, Riya assembled a team of passionate designers with a shared mission: to make every Indian home a reflection of its owner's aspirations.

What began as a modest two-person team has grown into a 25-member powerhouse of interior designers, architects, project managers, and craftspeople who have collectively transformed over 500 homes across India.

Today, HomeTrendzz is recognized as one of the most trusted interior design companies in North India, having won 20+ design awards and serving clients across Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Pune, and beyond.
