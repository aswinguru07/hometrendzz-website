Common Questions

Pricing FAQs

What does "per sq.ft." pricing mean? +
The per sq.ft. price is calculated based on the built-up area of the rooms being designed (not the entire apartment area). For example, a 200 sq.ft. living room at ₹1,799/sq.ft. = ₹3,59,800 for that room's complete interior.
Are there any hidden charges? +
Absolutely not. We provide a fully itemized quotation before any work begins. If scope changes, we discuss and agree in writing before proceeding. No nasty surprises.
Can I pay in installments? +
Yes! We offer flexible payment schedules: typically 30% on contract signing, 40% at execution start, and 30% on project completion. Premium package clients can also opt for EMI options.
How long does a full home interior project take? +
A standard 2BHK typically takes 45–60 days. A full 3BHK takes 60–90 days. Larger homes or premium finishes may take 90–120 days. We provide a detailed timeline in the project proposal.
What if I want a mix of features from different packages? +
No problem at all! We can create a custom package tailored exactly to your needs. Get in touch and our design consultant will work out the perfect solution for you.
